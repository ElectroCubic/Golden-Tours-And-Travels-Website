HIGHLIGHTS OF EUROPE 8 Days

ENGLAND - London 2 Nights, FRANCE - Paris 1 Night, SWITZERLAND - Lucerne 2 Nights, Engelberg
ITALY - Pisa, Florence 1 Night, Rome 1 Night.

Day 1: London

Arrive in London in the evening.

Day 2: London

City orientation tour includes Tower Bridge, 'Changing The Guard' ceremony at Buckingham Palace (subject to operation), Piccadilly Circus, Westminster Abbey, Big Ben, Trafalgar Square, Houses of Parliament, Hyde Park. Enjoy the London Eye ride.

Day 3: London - Paris

Cross the English channel while you travel by Eurostar from London to Paris. Arrive in Paris. City orientation tour includes Champs Elysees, L'Arc de Triomphe and Place de la Concorde. Visit the Eiffel Tower - top level to get a panoramic view of Paris.

Day 4: Paris - Lucerne

Proceed to Lucerne. Arrive in Lucerne in the evening.

Day 5: Lucerne - Mt. Titlis - Lucerne

Visit Engelberg - Mt. Titlis to experience 'Titlis Rotair' - World's 1st Revolving cable car ride to Mt. Titlis and visit Ice Grotto, thrilling Ice Flyer ride and Snow sliding. Lucerne city orientation tour includes Lion Monument and Kapplebrucke Wooden Bridge. Free time for shopping.

Day 6: Lucerne - Pisa - Florence

Proceed to Pisa, on arrival visit Square of Miracles, Baptistry, Cathedral and Leaning tower of Pisa. Later proceed to Florence. On arrival enjoy photostop at Piazzale Michelangelo Point and Statue of David in Florence.

Day 7: Florence - Rome

Proceed to Rome. On arrival, city orientation tour includes Tiber river, Roman Forum, Trevi Fountain,
Piazza Venezia, Circus Maximus and a photostop at Colosseum from outside.

Day 8: Departure from Rome

Board the flight for your hometown. Tour concludes.
