Travelling Essentials

  1. These are the things which you should ALWAYS carry with you while Travelling =>

    1. Passport / VISA
    2. Aadhar Card / Voter's ID Card
    3. Necessary Clothing & Medicines
    4. Digital Camera with Extra Batteries
    5. Comfortable Footwear / Sports Shoes
    6. Hand Sanitizer

  2. These are the things which are HELPFUL while Travelling =>

    • Sunscreen Lotion (SPF 30+)
    • Sunglasses / Reading glasses
    • Umbrella
    • Warm Woollen Clothes
    • Water Bottles and Napkins

  3. These are the things which you should NOT CARRY with you while Travelling =>

    • Gold / Diamond Jewellry
    • Expensive Cameras or Handbags
    • Expensive Smartphones and Laptops
    • Any other Valuable Items like Watches and Necklaces Etc.
